- `basics.py` is the leaf utility layer: pure-tensor math (quaternion ops, SH↔RGB, projection matrix), two dataclasses (`dataclass_camera`, `dataclass_gs`) used as render inputs, and optimizer-mutating helpers (`remove_from_optim`, `dup_in_optim`) for densification.
- `vanilla_render.py` defines the base renderer class `VanillaGaussians_render`; `vanilla.py` subclasses it into `VanillaGaussians`, implementing standard 3DGS initialization from PCD, per-step refinement (split/cull via grad norms + screen-size thresholds), and parameter-group construction.
- `pvg.py` builds `PeriodicVibrationGaussians` by multiple inheritance on `PeriodicVibrationGaussians_render` and `VanillaGaussians`, adding time-aware parameters `_taus`, `_velocity`, `_betas` and overriding `after_train`/`refinement_after` to split on both spatial and temporal gradients.
- `deformgs.py` provides `DeformableGaussians(VanillaGaussians)` that wraps a `DeformNetwork` (from `..modules`) to predict per-frame delta_xyz/delta_quat/delta_scale; deformation is gated by `ctrl_cfg.coarse_train_interval`.
- `scaffold.py` holds `ScaffoldGaussians(nn.Module)`, a thin container over ctrl/reg configs and scene scale intended to orchestrate multiple Gaussians instances.
- `__init__.py` exposes a runtime switch: setting env var `GS_MODE=render` rebinds `PeriodicVibrationGaussians` / `VanillaGaussians` to their `*_render` counterparts, otherwise imports the training variants; `deformgs.DeformableGaussians` is currently commented out in both branches.
