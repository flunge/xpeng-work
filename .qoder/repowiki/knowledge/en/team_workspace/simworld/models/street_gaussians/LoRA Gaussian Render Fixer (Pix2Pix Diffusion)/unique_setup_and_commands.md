Pretrained base model must be placed under `--base-model-id` (e.g. `/workspace/.../street_gaussians_render_fix`) and downloaded via `export HF_ENDPOINT=https://hf-mirror.com` before running. Training is launched through `fuyao_run/run_train.sh`, which loops over pickled dataset dicts under `generative_model_data/origin/*` and calls `python train.py --base-model-id ... --dataset-dict-path ... --max-step 5000 --num_epoch 60 --dropout_prob 0.05 --save-dir ...`. Inference uses `python inference.py --base-model-id ... --lora-weights-path ... --inference-pkl-path ... --data-root ...`.
