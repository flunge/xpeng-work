Must run inside `nvcr.io/nvidia/cosmos/cosmos-predict2-container:1.2`; before training apply `patch /usr/local/lib/python3.12/dist-packages/cosmos_predict2/tokenizers/tokenizer.py tokenizer.patch`. Pretrained weights are downloaded via `hf download nvidia/Fixer --local-dir models`. Multi-GPU training is launched through `deploy/run_train_nvfixer_ngpu.sh <ngpus> <nodes> <port> <config_name>` which reads all hyperparameters from `debug_configs/<config>.yaml` and calls `accelerate launch --multi_gpu ... src/train_nvfixer.py --config ...`.
