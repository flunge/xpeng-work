Three-layer layout: `model/` holds the PyTorch `nn.Module` (`EvolSplatModel` in `model.py`) plus its sub-networks — `Projector` (2D windowed sampling with occlusion-aware visibility), `SparseCostRegNet` + helpers in `sparse_conv.py` (voxelization of seed points into a sparse tensor, 3D residual encoder-decoder on `torchsparse`, dense volume reconstruction), `MLP` / `Embedding` for SH coefficient prediction, scale/rotation/opacity/offset heads, and `base_field_component`. `train/` wraps training: `Trainer` orchestrates per-scene data loading, loss composition (L1 + SSIM + entropy), PSNR/SSIM metrics, TensorBoard logging, GradScaler-based optimization, and checkpoint save/load; `config.py` / `optimizers.py` provide OmegaConf-backed config and grouped optimizers. `utils/` is pure helper code (camera math, COLMAP parsing, tensor dataclass). Top-level `data_manager.py` and `dataset.py` assemble batches from scene folders (source/target images, poses, depths, segmentation masks) and feed them to `get_outputs`. `infer_evolsplat.py` is the standalone inference entry point that loads a pre-trained `.ckpt`, initializes the volume once via `init_volume()`, then iterates frames. Dependency direction is strictly one-way: trainer → data_manager → dataset/utils → model → sub-modules; no cross-imports back into train/data.
