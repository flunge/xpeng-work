Data pipeline is two-stage: first `python scripts/json_dataset.py --data_root <path> --output ./data/metadata.json` to scan scenes and emit a full metadata list, then `python scripts/split_dataset.py --input_json ./data/metadata.json --test_split 0.05 --seed 42` to carve train/test splits. Training is launched through `bash scripts/run.sh --mode train [section.key=value ...]` which wraps `accelerate launch train.py --config configs/train_lora_1.3b.yaml`; inference uses `bash scripts/run.sh --mode infer --run_dir <output_dir>` (or directly `inference_causal_test.py --run_dir ... --metadata_json ...`). Multi-GPU inference is orchestrated by `scripts/deploy_ngpu.bash` / `deploy_infer.bash` which submit separate `torchrun` jobs per GPU with distinct ports.
