- Optional CUDA-dependent packages are imported inside try/except blocks at module top and exposed as `*_AVAILABLE` flags (e.g. `FUSED_SSIM_AVAILABLE`, `SPARSE_ADAM_AVAILABLE`) that gate feature branches in training and rendering.
- Command-line parameters are declared declaratively through subclasses of `ParamGroup` in `arguments/__init__.py`, where attribute names starting with `_` become short flags and `extract()` produces a lightweight `GroupParams` namespace passed down the call chain.
- Learnable state in `GaussianModel` is stored as private `nn.Parameter` tensors prefixed with `_` and accessed through `@property` getters (`get_xyz`, `get_scaling`, `get_opacity`, …) that apply activation functions on the fly rather than storing activated copies.
- Training loop uses an EMA accumulator (`ema_loss_for_log`, `ema_Ll1depth_for_log`) updated every iteration and printed only every 10 steps, while periodic events (SH degree bump, densify/prune, opacity reset, checkpoint/save) are gated on explicit iteration modulo or membership checks against configured lists.
- Per-iteration diagnostics are collected into a `scalar_dict` and logged via TensorBoard's `SummaryWriter.add_scalar/add_images/histogram` when available, with graceful fallback when TensorBoard is absent.
