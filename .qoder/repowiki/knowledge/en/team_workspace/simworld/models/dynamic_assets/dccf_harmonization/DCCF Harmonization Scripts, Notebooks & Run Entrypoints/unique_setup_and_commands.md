Training/test flows are invoked exclusively through the shell scripts in `runs/` (e.g. `bash runs/train_dccf_idih.sh`, `bash runs/test_baselines_idih.sh`); each script hardcodes `CUDA_VISIBLE_DEVICES` and passes `--weights` pointing at checkpoints produced by the preceding stage. The notebook resize utilities expect an IHARM-style dataset rooted at `/S4/MI/xueb/data/IDIH/HAdobe5k` (path is hardcoded).
