Built on HuggingFace `diffusers` (StableDiffusionPipeline, AutoencoderKL, UNet2DConditionModel) with `stabilityai/sd-turbo` as the frozen base; training uses `accelerate` (bf16 mixed precision, optional xformers, gradient checkpointing), `peft` LoRA, `lpips` + VGG features for perceptual loss, and wandb/tensorboard logging. Inference supports TensorRT export (`export_vae_onnx_trt.py`, `fixerTrt.py`) and torch.compile warmup over resolution buckets.
