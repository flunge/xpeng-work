Training is launched via `python train_g3r.py --job_name <name> --region {ground|bkgd} [--checkpoint path]`; production jobs are submitted through `fuyao/deploy_g3r.bash <job_name> <region> <checkpoint>` which calls the Fuyao CLI against a fixed Docker image `infra-registry-vpc.cn-wulanchabu.cr.aliyuncs.com/data-infra/fuyao:yangxh7-240919-2046`. Training data is lazily downloaded at runtime by `down_training_data()` from `root_data_folder` listed in config.
