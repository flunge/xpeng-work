After Agent Pod restart run `bash /workspace/meal/setup.sh` once — it installs PyYAML, re-installs crontab entries (with UTC→Beijing offset comments), and starts the cron daemon. Cron jobs are installed in UTC: `0 10 * * *` = Beijing 18:00 daily notification, `0 12 28-31 * *` = month-end plan generation, `0 */2 * * *` = feedback check. Manual runs: `python3 scripts/generate_month.py --year 2026 --month 7`, `python3 scripts/notify_daily.py [--date YYYY-MM-DD]`, `python3 scripts/weekly_shop.py [--week N]`.
