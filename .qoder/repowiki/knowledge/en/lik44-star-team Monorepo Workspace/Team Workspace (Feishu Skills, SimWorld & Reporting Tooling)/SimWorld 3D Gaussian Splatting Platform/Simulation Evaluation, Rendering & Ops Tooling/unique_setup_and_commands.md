`deploy_render.bash` submits a Fuyao job that activates the `street-gaussians-ns` conda env, strips Qt5 ABI tags, and runs `tools/scripts/render_oss_mode.py`; `render_oss_mode.py` expects `USER_ROOT` and `REPO_ROOT` constants pointing at a local checkout of `codes/3dgs/` and uses `download_file_from_oss2` from `pipeline/ucp`. The `train_and_render_fm/Dockerfile` requires the repo tree to be copied into `/root/install` so it can pip-install local `.whl` and `street_gaussians/submodules/*` packages.
