Environment is declared in `mvsa/environment.yml` (conda) and `Makefile` targets build the custom marching-cubes CUDA extension before use. Training is launched through `python train.py --config_file <model_yaml> --data_config <data_yaml> --gpus N`, while inference/fusion runs via `python test.py --load_weights_from_checkpoint <ckpt> --config_file <model_yaml> --data_config <test_yaml>` or end-to-end using the XPeng-specific `configs/run/xpeng_run_config.yaml` passed as `--config_file`. Data preparation is driven by standalone scripts under `scripts/data_scripts/` (e.g. `generate_train_tuples_geometry.py`, `precompute_valid_frames.py`) and the XPeng assembler `utils/generate_data.py`.
