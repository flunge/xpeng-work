Two parallel model stacks sit under this leaf, both built on Detectron2 and registered via `@META_ARCH_REGISTRY.register()`:
- `Mask2Former/` — the base Mask2Former codebase plus a `mask2former_video/` extension that adds video-aware data (`ytvis.py`, `dataset_mapper.py`, `augmentation.py`), a `VideoSetCriterion` / `VideoHungarianMatcher`, a `VideoMaskFormer` meta-arch in `video_maskformer_model.py`, and a `transformer_decoder/video_mask2former_transformer_decoder.py`. Backbones include Swin and two adapter variants (`backbones_samAdapter/`, `backbones_vitAdapter/`) wrapping SAM/ViT encoders; pixel decoder ships a custom C++/CUDA `ms_deform_attn` op.
- `LOMM/` — an NVIDIA MinVIS-derived tracker layered on top of Mask2Former. `lomm/lomm.py` registers four meta-archs (`MinVIS`, `LOMM_online_E`, `LOMM_online`, `LOMM_offline`) that reuse `mask2former_video`'s matcher/criterion/backbone/head pipeline and add a `LOMM_Tracker`, `TemporalRefiner`, and a `VideoMultiScaleMaskedTransformerDecoder_minvis` decoder variant. Config is split across `lomm/config.py` (`add_lomm_config`, `add_minvis_config`, `add_dvis_config`) and per-dataset YAML trees under `LOMM/configs/{ovis,ytvis19,ytvis21,ytvis22}/{Base-*, LOMM_*, MinVIS_*}.yaml`.
- Top-level helpers (`clip_utils.py`, `dataset_utils.py`, `file_utils.py`) bridge to Xpeng's `xdata.dataset_v2.DatasetLoader`, exposing `prepare_dataset`, `get_dataset_clips`, `merge_subrun_jsons`, and clip validation routines used by downstream mining scripts.
