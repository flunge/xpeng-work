Development scripts under `scripts/`: `setup-dev-environment.sh` installs deps and creates `.venv`, `install-lark-cli.sh` provisions the local `lark-cli` used by `FeishuCliAuthAdapter`, `start_all.sh` boots the FastAPI app plus event bridge and local agent, while `start_event_bridge.sh` and `start_local_agent.sh` launch sibling processes. Runtime config comes from `agents/.env` (see `.env.example`).
