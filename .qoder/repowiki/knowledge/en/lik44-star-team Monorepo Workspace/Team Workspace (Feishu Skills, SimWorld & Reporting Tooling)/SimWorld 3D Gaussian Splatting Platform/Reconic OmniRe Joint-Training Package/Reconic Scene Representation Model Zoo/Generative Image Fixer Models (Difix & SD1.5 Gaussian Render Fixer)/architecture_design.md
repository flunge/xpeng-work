Two sibling `nn.Module` classes expose the same training/inference contract expected by the joint trainer:
- `difix_model.DifixModel`: inference-only wrapper around a pre-trained `DifixPipeline`. It lazily constructs the pipeline in `_get_pipe`, supports dual-resolution bucketing from a YAML `train_config`, optionally runs GroundingDINO+SAM for sky masks, and provides no-op `training_forward`/`save_checkpoint`/`resume_from_checkpoint`.
- `sd15_gaussian_fix_model.SD15_GaussianFixModel`: trainable SD1.5 model built from `diffusers` (`AutoencoderKL`, `UNet2DConditionModel`, `DDPMScheduler`, `CLIPTextModel`). The UNet's input channels are expanded to concatenate image conditioning with noise latents; VAE/text encoder are frozen while the UNet is trained with AdamW (optionally 8-bit via bitsandbytes) under `accelerate.Accelerator`. A prompt embed cache avoids re-tokenizing repeated prompts.

Both models share common helpers: center-crop resize (`_resize_image` / `_reverse_resize_image`), lazy pipeline construction guarded by `self.pipe is None`, and a `set_eval` that spins up a background `log_worker` thread to keep logs flowing during compilation. They depend on sibling packages `...pipelines.pipeline_difix`, `...pipelines.pipeline_gaussian_render_fixer`, and `...utils.model_utils` (GroundingDINOPipeline, SAMPipeline); nothing inside this module imports back into the parent `reconic.models` package, keeping the dependency direction one-way toward pipelines/utils.
